WestLife

West life are an Irish boys band, formed in 1998 in sligo, Ireland.The band disbanded in 2012 and reunited in 2018.The group has atotal of fourteen major album releases, twelve of which are studio albums. Five albums are a five-piece and eight are a four-piece. The group rose to fame with their debute international self-titled studio album,Westlife(1999) followed by coast to coast(2000), world of our own (2001) The group was originaly signed by Simon cowell in the UK, Clive Davis in the US and managed by Louis Walsh. The Westlife band was originaly called westside before changing to its current name and it consisted of:Shane Filan, Markus Feehily, Kian Egan, Nick Byrne and Brian McFadden who departed from the group after the Turnaround Tour in 2004.
